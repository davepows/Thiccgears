Common Questions

We've Heard It All

Why are there two C's in ThiccGears?
One C was not enough. Three C's looked like a typo. Two C's is the internet's way of saying you mean it. We mean it.
Are these gears FRC legal?
We've submitted the paperwork. FIRST is reviewing it. The question on the table is whether "gear" includes "structural element" at 31mm. Legal says probably. Probably.
Will thick gears make my robot slower?
Almost certainly. But speed is a metric. Presence is a vibe. ThiccGears sells vibes. Measure what matters to you.
How do you feel about ThinGears?
We respect ThinGears deeply. Friendly rivalry. We once mailed them one of our Absolute Unit gears as a gift. They have not responded. We understand.
Is "Beefy Grade" and "Unhinged Grade" a real aluminum classification?
It is a ThiccGears internal classification system. The underlying alloy is standard 6061. The grade names reflect our values, not the alloy. This is called brand integrity.
